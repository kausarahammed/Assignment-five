Aid for Injured in the Quota Movement

Quota movement in Bangladesh refers to a series of protests and social movements aimed at reforming or abolishing the government-imposed quota system in public sector jobs and university admissions. This movement primarily gained momentum in 2018, but the issue had been a point of contention for years. The Quota Movement was one of the largest student-led protests in Bangladesh's recent history. It highlighted issues of fairness, transparency, and the role of quotas in ensuring equal opportunity. The movement bout the balance between affirmative action and meritocracy in the context of national development.
